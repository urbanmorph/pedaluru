Established by the United Nations, highlights the significant advantages of cycling for individuals, communities, and the environment. This global recognition by the UN underscores the positive impact of cycling worldwide.

About Pedaluru

Pedaluru is an annual cycling initiative held in Bengaluru on World Bicycle Day. This event aims to promote health and sustainability by encouraging the community to embrace cycling.

Pedaluru celebrates the joy of riding and advocates for active mobility in the city. It highlights the benefits of cycling for individuals and the environment, emphasizing the importance of green transportation.

Experience the city from a new perspective and enjoy the freedom of cycling while supporting a sustainable urban lifestyle.

Join us for Pedaluru as we pedal towards a greener and healthier Bengaluru!
